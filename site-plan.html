Overview

Purpose

I'm a singer with a passion for video game soundtracks, and I've made a hobby of writing lyrics for some of these instrumentals. I'm planning to create a place to store all the lyrics I've written for easy reading, and a place to also store the covers I sing with the lyrics. Is YouTube organized enough for this? Not if I plan to include easy reading and a list of what covers are coming up in the future. People can view my work and see it nicely sorted.

Audience

I intend for my audience to be my friends, irl and online, and those who watch my channel or follow me elsewhere. As my site is planned to be a place for people to more easily view my works, it only makes sense that the audience would be those who view my works.
